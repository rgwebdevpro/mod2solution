Neopolitan Root Menu

Chocolate

Spanish, from Nahuatl chocolātl, probably alteration of eastern Nahuatl dialect chikolātl, from chikolli hook

Strawberry

Middle English, from Old English strēawberige, from strēaw straw and berige berry

Vanilla

New Latin, from Spanish vainilla vanilla (plant and fruit), diminutive of vaina sheath, from Latin vagina sheath, vagina
